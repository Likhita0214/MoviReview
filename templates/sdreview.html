May be he is too sentimental but this developer sorry! Devloveper never disappoints you!

Rating:3.5/5

  Software Developer is story of an aspirant youngster shannu (played by shanmukh jaswanth) who wants to develop in his career .He is currently working as project head in a Software Company and only once again we repeat he only focus on his professional career .The team were his friends who were not like their project head balances both their professional and personal life. His team-mates always makes the plans to keep themselves enjoying in their free time. But our hero always try to engage in some sort of work. What if someone enter into his life to make his routine upside down? What if Mr Shannu have turned into a lover boy from a true gentlemen? To know That you have to watch Software Devloveper series.
