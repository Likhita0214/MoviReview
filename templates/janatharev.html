Movies/Janatha Garage



Cast : Jr.NTR,MohanLal,Samantha,NityaMenon
Written & Directed by : Koratala Siva
Editor : Kotagiri Venkateswara Rao
Cinematography : Tirru
Music Director : Devi Sri Prasad
Release date : 1 September 2016 Available-in : Disney+Hotstar
Watch now @

This People's Garage has a mix of all generes family,message oriented,action and everything

Rating:3.5/5

  A godfather-esque figure, a defiant family member, a gang of obedient followers, hundreds of people coming for help and enemies looking for a way to end this reign, and an heir apparent. Not a new story for sure, and yet, Janatha Garage manages to make a mark with a bit of novelty. With several pages taken from The Godfather, this film takes you into the world of two spirited men – Anand (NTR) and Satyam (Mohanlal) – who’d give away all they have just for the cause they believe in. A man who loves the earth’s environment joins a man who loves the beings on earth in a mission, and the result leaves us with mixed feelings.
  The highlight scenes in the film would be Anand standing by a government employee (Rajeev Kanakala) who refuses to be corrupt, Raghava’s (Unni Mukundan) final plea to his father (Mohanlal), Samantha and NTR’s moment of separation which will make you shed a tear, among several others. But the interesting thing about all these moments is how they stand out only because of the performances of the Mohanlal and NTR who outperform each other at every instance.
